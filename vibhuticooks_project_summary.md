# VibhutiCooks.com Project Context & Game Plan

This document serves as the master reference for the **Vibhuti Cooks** landing page design and strategy.

## 1. Brand Identity & Positioning
* **Domain:** vibhuticooks.com
* **Tagline:** *Your Kitchen. My Culinary Canvas.*
* **Core Value Proposition:** High-end, private chef services focused on health-conscious techniques, premium ingredients (cold-pressed oils, quality sourcing), and global culinary versatility.
* **The "Specialist but Global" Strategy:** Highlight heritage roots (Nepali and Indian cuisine) while positioning the chef as a versatile global professional capable of Mediterranean, Modern American, and health-focused (Keto/Vegan) menus.

## 2. Target Audience
* **Busy Families:** Professional households in need of nutritious, high-quality meal prep.
* **Couples:** Those looking for an intimate, restaurant-grade "Date Night" experience at home.
* **Elderly Folks:** Individuals needing quality-conscious, easy-to-digest, healthy meals and a helping hand around the kitchen.

## 3. Geographic Targeting (Maryland)
Focus areas for SEO and service delivery:
* Frederick (Home base)
* Rockville
* Columbia
* Ellicott City

## 4. Visual Language & UX
* **Style:** Modern, Minimalistic, and Vibrant.
* **Palette:** "Gallery White" backgrounds, Deep Emerald Green (Health), and Saffron/Terracotta (Spice/Flavor) accents.
* **Key Features:**
    * Mobile-first, touch-responsive design.
    * **Swiper.js** carousel for food photography.
    * **AOS (Animate On Scroll)** for smooth, premium transitions.
    * Sticky "Call to Action" for mobile users.

## 5. Site Structure (Single Page)
1.  **Hero:** Minimalist greeting with the "Culinary Canvas" tagline.
2.  **The Conscious Kitchen:** Section detailing the focus on health, oils, and ingredient quality.
3.  **Visual Portfolio:** A modern carousel showing diverse dishes (Himalayan Thali next to Pan-Seared Sea Bass).
4.  **Service Packages:** * Weekly Meal Prep (Hourly placeholder).
    * Specialty Events/Date Nights (Bespoke).
    * Golden Years Service (Nutritious elderly care).
5.  **Booking Form:** Captures Name, Area (Dropdown), Address, and Dietary Goals.

## 6. Technical Stack
* **Frontend:** HTML5, Tailwind CSS, Vanilla JavaScript.
* **Libraries:** Swiper.js (Sliders), AOS.js (Animations), Google Fonts (Playfair Display & Inter).

## 7. Future Call to Actions (CTAs) to Rotate
* *Primary:* "Reserve Your Date"
* *Secondary:* "Start Your Healthy Eating Journey"
* *Action:* "Check Availability"
